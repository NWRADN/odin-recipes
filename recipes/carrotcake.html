Carrot Cake

Moist and flavorful carrot cake

This carrot cake is soft, spiced, and topped with a rich cream cheese frosting.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups grated carrots
  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 1/2 cup brown sugar
  • 3 eggs
  • 1 cup vegetable oil
  • 2 tsp baking powder
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • 1 tsp cinnamon
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 175°C (350°F).
  2. Mix the dry ingredients in a bowl.
  3. Whisk eggs, sugar, oil, and vanilla together.
  4. Fold in grated carrots.
  5. Combine wet and dry ingredients.
  6. Pour into a baking pan and bake for 40–45 minutes.
